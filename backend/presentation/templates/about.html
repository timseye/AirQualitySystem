About AAQIS

Air Quality Intelligence System for Astana

AAQIS is a comprehensive system for monitoring, analyzing, and visualizing air quality data in Astana, Kazakhstan.

Project Goals
  • Provide accurate short-term (24–48 hours) forecasts of PM2.5 concentrations
  • Identify seasonal and diurnal pollution patterns
  • Deliver clear visualizations through an accessible web interface
Data Sources
Source Data Type Coverage
OpenAQ PM2.5 measurements (U.S. Embassy station) 2016–2024
Open-Meteo Weather data (temperature, humidity, wind) 2016–2024
CAMS (Copernicus) Atmospheric composition reanalysis 2024

Academic Context

This system was developed as part of a diploma thesis at Astana IT University, Department of Computer Science.

Thesis Title: "Development of a System for Analyzing and Visualizing Air Quality in Astana Using Data Analysis Techniques"
